About me

  • Research interests

    My interest is in the methods used to tackle the quantum many-body problem, specifically in the realm of electronic structure. I seek to understand and develop insightful approaches, and test approximations by writing code.

    In addition to original research papers, I write articles to explain the theories and approximations in use. See my Publications. I may also blog about some interesting theories from time to time.

  • Programming interests

    I enjoy coding and pretending to make games or other useful content. Check out my Coding webpage. Various "helpful" code-snippets may also appear on my blog, in addition to other things.

  • Loving justice, mercy, and walking humbly

    ... is the life-goal, but not something I do do very well naturally. For a long time I thought I knew God, and yet was so far from being blameless, or even trusting God. I realized that with the help of some friends and divine lighting. I found both mercy and conviction in the once and future king, Jesus.
