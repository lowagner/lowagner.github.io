  • Research interests

    My interest is in the methods used to tackle the quantum many-body problem, specifically in the realm of electronic structure. I seek to understand and develop insightful approaches, and test approximations by writing code.

    In addition to original research papers, I write articles to explain the theories and approximations in use. See my publications, specifically DFT in a nutshell.

  • Creations

    In addition to coding for research, I enjoy attempting to make games and other useful content. Check out my coding webpage for links of what I've done in the past and what I'm working on now. I support open source programming, so most of the releasable code is here on github. Various "helpful" code-snippets may also appear on my blog, in addition to other things.

    Ages ago I wrote some electronic music, which may sound appealing to some (and a little grating on others).
