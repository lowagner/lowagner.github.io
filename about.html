  • Research interests

    I have pursued some unique and interesting ideas to solve the quantum many-body problem, specifically electronic structure using density functional theory.

      

    In addition to original research papers, I've written articles to explain the theories and approximations in use. See my publications, specifically DFT in a nutshell, which made the cover art!
